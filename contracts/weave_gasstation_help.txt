Gas Station subcommands.

See 'https://docs.initia.xyz/developers/developer-guides/tools/clis/weave-cli/gas-station' for more information about the setup process and potential issues.

Usage:
  weave gas-station [command]

Available Commands:
  setup       Setup Gas Station account on Initia and Celestia for funding the OPinit-bots or relayer to send transactions
  show        Show Initia and Celestia Gas Station addresses and balances

Flags:
  -h, --help   help for gas-station

Use "weave gas-station [command] --help" for more information about a command.
